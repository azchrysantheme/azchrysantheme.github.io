WHO ARE YA?!

Ah, the age-old question! I'm a queer artist who somehow managed to escape a life of... well, not making things. My primary mode of existence involves wrangling electronic sounds out of synths, a process that requires the patience of a saint and the technical skills of someone who occasionally plugs things into the wrong hole (don't tell anyone). It's a journey filled with unexpected beeps, glorious accidents, and the occasional moment where I think I've created the next masterpiece, only to realize it sounds like a dying robot gargling static.

Beyond the sonic realm, I also wield the mighty pen (or stylus, let's be real) to conjure forth drawings. Sometimes they're intricate, sometimes they're just silly doodles that make me giggle.
